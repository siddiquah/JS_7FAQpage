Frequently Asked Questions

What is the story of Naruto?

The story of Naruto follows the life of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja with a powerful demon fox sealed inside him. Naruto dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village, and embarks on a journey to become the strongest ninja and gain the respect of his fellow villagers.

What is the Akatsuki in Naruto?

The Akatsuki is a criminal organization made up of powerful ninja in the Naruto series. Their goal is to capture all of the tailed beasts, including the demon fox inside Naruto, in order to achieve world domination. They are the main antagonists of Naruto Shippuden.

Is Itachi Uchiha evil

Itachi Uchiha is initially portrayed as an evil character who killed his entire clan, including his parents and younger brother, Sasuke. Later in the series, it is revealed that Itachi had a secret mission to protect the Hidden Leaf Village by infiltrating the Akatsuki and gathering intelligence. He also had a deep love for his brother, Sasuke, and sacrificed his reputation and relationship with him in order to keep him safe. Itachi is ultimately shown to be a complex character with a tragic past, and whether he is considered evil or not is up to individual interpretation.
